Tasty Lentil Tacos

This fun taco dish is both healthy and delicious. It will be a hit with the entire family! Serve with your favorite taco fillings, such as sour cream, chopped tomatoes, shredded lettuce, and cheese.
Image Source

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

40 min

Servings

6

Nutrition Info

  • 2 Tacos: 365 calories
  • 12g fat (5g saturated fat)
  • 21mg cholesterol
  • 777mg sodium
  • 44g carbohydrates
  • 19g protein

Ingredients

  1. 1 teaspoon canola oil
  2. 1 medium onion - finely chopped
  3. 1 garlic clove - minced
  4. 1 cup dried lentils - rinsed
  5. 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  6. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  7. 2.5 cups vegetable or reducted-sodium chicken broth
  8. 1 cup salsa
  9. 12 taco shells
  10. 1.5 cups shredded lettuce
  11. 1 cup chopped fresh tomatoes
  12. 1.5 cups shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese
  13. 6 tablespoons fat-free sour cream

Enjoy!
